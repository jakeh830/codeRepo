9 Classes

The Scout

From Boston. Is very fast. Enjoys calling people “chuckle-nuts”. Is currently running circles around you. Often wears lime green and taunts after kills. Has a bucket of fried chicken. Often exclaims that the current topic of interest “sucks on ice”.
